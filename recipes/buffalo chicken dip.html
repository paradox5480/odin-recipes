Buffalo Chicken Dip

This tangy, creamy Buffalo chicken dip tastes just like Buffalo chicken wings! Perfect for parties and game day, this chicken dip is best served hot with crackers and celery sticks. Everyone loves the results!

Ingredients

    Chicken
    Cream cheese
    Ranch dressing
    Hot sauce
    Cheese

How to Make Buffalo Chicken Dip:

    Heat the chicken and hot sauce in a skillet until heated through, then mix in the cream cheese and dressing.
    When mixture is well blended, add half of the cheese and stir. Transfer dip into slow cooker, sprinkling remaining cheese on the top.
    Cook until bubbly.
